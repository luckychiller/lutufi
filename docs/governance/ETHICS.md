# Ethical Framework & Use Policy: Lutufi

## Core Principles
Lutufi is a tool of immense power, capable of modeling influence, surveillance, and systemic risk. Its development is guided by the following principles:

1. **Academic Transparency:** All code and methodologies must be open for audit and peer review.
2. **Reproducibility:** A result that cannot be independently verified is not a scientific result.
3. **Harm Prevention:** We do not build features explicitly designed for deceptive manipulation or unauthorized surveillance.

## Misuse Scenarios & Design Responses
### 1. Surveillance & De-anonymization
**Concern:** Lutufi could be used to infer the identity or sensitive attributes of individuals in anonymized networks.
**Response:** Lutufi's documentation will include best practices for differential privacy and explicit warnings about the limits of anonymization in probabilistic networks.

### 2. Influence Operations
**Concern:** The library could be used by authoritarian regimes to optimize the spread of propaganda or detect dissent.
**Response:** We maintain an open, public record of contributors and prioritize features that detect and quantify misinformation rather than those that optimize its spread.

### 3. Financial Manipulation
**Concern:** Traders could use Lutufi to identify and exploit systemic weaknesses in financial markets.
**Response:** By making Lutufi open-source, we ensure that regulators and systemic risk researchers have access to the same (or better) tools as market participants, leveling the informational playing field.

## Non-Legally Binding Ethic
Lutufi is designed for:
- Understanding how to stop diseases from spreading.
- Identifying and mitigating systemic financial risks.
- Improving information flow in organizations.
- Researching the evolution of social norms and beliefs.

It is NOT designed for:
- Targeted harassment or surveillance of individuals.
- Optimizing deceptive psychological operations.
- Undermining democratic processes through covert influence.

## Call for Accountability
We encourage any user who identifies a feature or use case of Lutufi that violates human rights or causes systemic harm to report it to the maintainers for open, public discussion.
