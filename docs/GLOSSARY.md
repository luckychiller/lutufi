# Glossary of Core Terms: Lutufi

## Probability & Inference
- **Bayesian Network:** A probabilistic graphical model that represents a set of variables and their conditional dependencies via a directed acyclic graph (DAG).
- **Conditional Independence:** A state where two variables are independent given the state of a third variable.
- **Belief Propagation:** A message-passing algorithm for performing inference on graphical models.
- **Inference:** The process of calculating the posterior distribution of variables given observed evidence.
- **Exact Inference:** Calculating the exact probability distribution (e.g., Junction Tree algorithm).
- **Approximate Inference:** Estimating the distribution through sampling or optimization (e.g., MCMC, Loopy Belief Propagation).
- **Priors:** The initial probability distribution of a variable before evidence is observed.

## Network Science
- **Structural Network:** A set of nodes (actors) and edges (relationships/ties) representing connectivity.
- **Homophily:** The tendency of individuals to associate and bond with similar others.
- **Centrality:** A measure of the relative importance of a node within a network structure.
- **Contagion:** The spread of a behavior, belief, or state through a network.
- **Dynamic Network:** A network where nodes and edges change state, appear, or disappear over time.
- **Multiplex Network:** A network containing multiple types of relationships (e.g., professional, social, and financial) between the same set of nodes.

## Economic & Social Specifics
- **Systemic Risk:** The risk of collapse of an entire system as opposed to risk associated with any one individual component.
- **Information Asymmetry:** A situation in which one party in a relationship has more or better information than the other.
- **Strategic Interaction:** Situations where the outcome for an actor depends on the actions of others in the network.

## Lutufi Specifics
- **Lutufi Model:** A unified representation containing both network structure and its corresponding probabilistic parameters.
- **Inference Engine:** The core module responsible for executing mathematical calculations.
- **Domain Example:** A self-contained research case study demonstrating Lutufi's application to a specific field.
